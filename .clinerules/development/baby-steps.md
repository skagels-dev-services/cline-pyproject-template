# Baby Steps Methodology

## The Six Rules

1. **The Smallest Possible Meaningful Change**
   Always break down tasks into the smallest possible meaningful change. Never attempt to accomplish multiple things at once.

2. **The Process is the Product**
   The journey of learning and execution is more important than the final destination.

3. **One Substantive Accomplishment at a Time**
   Focus on one, and only one, substantive accomplishment at a time.

4. **Complete Each Step Fully**
   Ensure each step is brought to a state of completion before starting the next.

5. **Incremental Validation is Mandatory**
   Validate work after every single step. Do not assume a change works - verify it.

6. **Document Every Step with Focus**
   Document every change with specific, focused detail. Documentation is as critical as the code.
